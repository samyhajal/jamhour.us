RAYMOND DEBBANE:
Raymond Debbane is President and Chief Executive Officer of Invus, an investment firm which he co-founded in 1985 in New York as the exclusive investment advisor of Benelux-based Artal Group S.A.
Invus is a global investment firm with over $4B in capital, with offices in New York, Paris and Hong Kong. The primary focus is on private equity investments including LBO's, early stage venture capital and expansion financings. Invus also manages a concentrated public stocks portfolio as well as a fund of hedge funds
Invus has been an equity investor in companies seeking to transform their industries across a wide range of sectors including consumer products and services, food, specialty retail, software, biotech, medical devices and products and services to professionals.
Invus partners with owner-managers of private and public companies to help them achieve extraordinary business performance. Over its 25+ year history, Invus has achieved both cash on cash multiples and annual internal rates of return that are at the very top of the private equity industry.
Ray is the CEO of Artal Group and the Chairman of the Board of Weight Watchers International and of Lexicon Pharmaceuticals as well as a Board member of a number of private companies in the Artal/Invus portfolio. He formerly was on the Board of Keebler Foods. Ray is the Chairman of Action Against Hunger-USA and a Trustee Emeritus of Connecticut College.
Prior to forming Invus in 1985, Ray was a manager for The Boston Consulting Group in Paris. He holds an MBA from Stanford Graduate School of Business, an MS in Food Science and Technology from the University of California, Davis and a BS in Agricultural Sciences and Agricultural Engineering from the American University of Beirut.
