About Jamhour Alumni US

Jamhour Alumni, US Inc. is a non-profit alumni association organized in 2004 under New York State laws. It is a 501 c (3) non-profit, non political organization, founded by a group of Jamhour Alumni (Anciens de Jamhour) desiring to assist needy students attending their alma mater, ‘Collège Notre Dame de Jamhour’, in their home country of Lebanon. Since inception, and through 2013, above $1.6 million have been raised thanks to the generous contributions of its sponsors.

‘Collège Notre Dame de Jamhour’, is run by the Jesuit order of the Catholic Church. Gathered funds are used to cover the tuitions of deserving students unable to meet tuition expenses; more than 550 scholarships have been provided through 2013. Special fund raisings by either a particular ‘class’ or specific individuals have also targeted the creation of modern Computer and Science Laboratories.

The membership in the Jamhour Alumni, US Inc is a privilege extended only to former students from the ‘Collège Notre Dame de Jamhour’, its predecessor ‘The Collège of Saint Joseph’ and all membership holding alumni.

Elias Sayegh

Mr. Sayegh is an Associate Attorney in the New York office of King & Spalding LLP, an international law firm. His practice focuses on finance transactions including various types of acquisition financing and private equity work.

Prior to joining King & Spalding LLP, Elias was a Structured Finance Associate with BSEC - Bemo Securitisation SAL, in Beirut, Lebanon, where he helped structuring securitization transactions in the Middle East.

Elias graduated with a Masters of Arts in Law and Diplomacy from the Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy at Tufts University and holds a LL.M degree in Banking and Financial Law from Boston University Law School. He also holds a LL.B in French and Lebanese law and a B.S. in Political Science and Public Administration from Université Saint Joseph in Beirut. He is a member of the New York Bar and the Beirut Bar Association.

He graduated from Notre Dame de Jamhour in 1996 and has been a member of the board of Jamhour Alumni US since 2010.

Karim A. Awad

Mr. Awad attended Notre Dame de Jamhour until 1983 and graduated from the Lycée Janson de Sailly in Paris in 1987.

Karim holds an Engineering degree and an MBA, respectively, from The Catholic University of America and the American University both in Washington DC. He pursued a five-year career in management consulting with Accenture, and became involved in Asset Management in 2000. He is an alternative investments specialist, and in 2006 he founded Edgemore Capital – a firm that specializes in investment advisory, including the development of tailor-made products.

He has been on the board of the Jamhour Alumni US committee since 2004.

Gabriel A. Sara, M.D.

Dr. Sara graduated from Notre Dame de Jamhour in 1972. He later attended medical school at the Faculté Française de Médecine (FFM) in Beirut and at Paris XI School of Medicine to obtain his Doctorate in Medicine in 1980.

In 1980, he moved to New York City to complete his medical training. He holds American Boards in three fields: Internal Medicine, Hematology and Medical Oncology.

Gabriel is a Senior Attending Physician in the division of Hematology/Oncology at St. Luke’s-Roosevelt Hospital Center in New York and also an Assistant Clinical Professor in Medicine at the College of Physicians & Surgeons at Columbia University, New York.

In 2008, he became the Medical Director of the Chemotherapy Unit at the St. Luke’s-Roosevelt Hospital Center.

In 2003, Gabriel co-founded “Jamhour Alumni US” and was its President from 2006 to 2010.

He is married to Nada Abousleiman and they have three children: Albert (1982), Aline (1984) and Paul (1990).

Christian Rizk, M.D.

Dr. Rizk attended Notre Dame de Jamhour until 1970 and graduated from the Lycée Français de Beyrouth in 1973. He obtained his doctorate in Medicine in 1982 from Brussels Free University in Belgium.

He then went to New York where he completed his residency training in Pathology at St.Lukes Roosevelt Hospital Center and a Fellowship in Pathology and Immuno-hematopathology at NYU Medical center in New York city. He holds the American Board of Pathology. He is a Senior Attending staff at Cabrini Medical Center in New York.

He is married to Nada Rasamny and they have 2 children: Rhea (1995) and Alex (1998).

Nada Sara

Mrs. Sara graduated from the Institut Sainte Anne de Besançon of Beirut in 1974. She later attended law school at the Université Saint Joseph (USJ) in Beirut where she obtained her degree in Lebanese Law and Master’s in French Law in 1978. She recently went back to school in New York – where she has been living since 1981 – and joined the LL.M program at Fordham School of Law, graduating in 2007.

Nada has been involved in the Jamhour Alumni US association since its founding in 2003 and has become the main event planner for its yearly fund raiser.

She is married to Jamhour alumnus Gabriel Sara, and they have three children: Albert (1982), Aline (1984) and Paul (1990).
