Karim A. Awad

Mr. Awad attended Notre Dame de Jamhour until 1984 and graduated from Lycee Janson de Sailly in Paris in 1987. He then moved to Washington DC, where he graduated in 1990 from The Catholic University of America with a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering; he later received an MBA from the American University in Washington DC.

He started and pursued for 5 years a career in management consulting with Accenture, then founded and managed for 3 years Aeacus Capital, an asset management firm.

In 2003, he joined a start up hedge fund advisory firm in New York as an SVP of institutional sales, where he focused on providing customized alternative investments solutions. As of January 2007, Mr. Awad handles non-US investor relations for a 1.8 billion dollars Swiss-based hedge fund investments advisory firm.

Gabriel A. Sara, M.D.

Dr. Sara graduated from Notre Dame de Jamhour in 1972. He later attended medical school at the Faculté Française de Médecine (FFM) in Beirut and at Paris XI School of Medicine to obtain his Doctorate in Medicine in 1980.

In 1980, he moved to New York City to complete his medical training. He holds American Boards in three fields: Internal Medicine, Hematology and Medical Oncology.

Gabriel is a Senior Attending Physician in the division of Hematology/Oncology at St. Luke’s-Roosevelt Hospital Center in New York and also an Assistant Clinical Professor in Medicine at the College of Physicians & Surgeons at Columbia University, New York.

In 2008, he became the Medical Director of the Chemotherapy Unit at the St. Luke’s-Roosevelt Hospital Center.

In 2003, Gabriel co-founded “Jamhour Alumni US” and was its President from 2006 to 2010.

He is married to Nada Abousleiman and they have three children: Albert (1982), Aline (1984) and Paul (1990).

Christian Rizk, M.D.

Dr. Rizk attended Notre Dame de Jamhour until 1970 and graduated from the Lycee francais de Beyrouth in 1973. He obtained his doctorate in Medicine in 1982 form Brussels Free University in Belgium.

He then went to New York where he completed his residency training in Pathology at St.Lukes Roosevelt Hospital Center and a Fellowship in Pathology and Immuno-hematopathology at NYU Medical center in New York city. He holds the American Board of Pathology. He is a Senior Attending staff at Cabrini Medical Center in New York.

He is married to Nada Rasamny and they have 2 children: Rhea (1995) and Alex (1998).

Nada Sara

Mrs. Nada Sara graduated from the Institut Sainte Anne de Besançon of Beirut in 1974. She later attended law school at the Université Saint Joseph (USJ) in Beirut where she obtained her degree in Lebanese Law and Master’s in French Law in 1978. She recently went back to school in New York – where she has been living since 1981 – and joined the LL.M program at Fordham School of Law, graduating in 2007.

Nada has been involved in the Jamhour Alumni US association since its founding in 2003 and has become the main event planner for its yearly fund raiser.

She is married to Jamhour alumnus Gabriel Sara, and they have three children: Albert (1982), Aline (1984) and Paul (1990).

Elias Sayegh

Elias Sayegh is an Associate Attorney in the New York office of King & Spalding LLP, an international law firm. His practice focuses on finance transactions including various types of acquisition financings and private equity work.

Prior to joining King & Spalding LLP, he was a Structured Finance Associate with BSEC - Bemo Securitisation SAL, in Beirut, Lebanon, where he helped structuring securitization transactions in the Middle East.

Mr. Sayegh graduated with a Masters of Arts in Law and Diplomacy from the Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy at Tufts University and holds a LL.M degree in Banking and Financial Law from Boston University Law School. He also holds a LL.B in French and Lebanese law and a B.S. in Political Science and Public Administration from Université Saint Joseph in Beirut. Mr. Sayegh is a member of the New York Bar and the Beirut Bar Association.

He graduated from Notre Dame de Jamhour in 1996 and has been a member of the board of Jamhour Alumni US since 2010.

Bud Zehil

Bud Zehil is a co-founder of Jamhour Alumni US and a Board member. He is co-founder and President of AdTek Information Systems, Inc., a New York-based global industry leader providing integrated software products and customized software and Internet solutions to the financial services industry.
