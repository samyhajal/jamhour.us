Jamhour Alumni U.S., Inc. is a U.S.-based, non-profit corporation. Its mission is to arrange, sponsor and conduct fundraising events – and collect donations and charitable contributions – in favor of Collège Notre Dame de Jamhour located in Jamhour, Lebanon.

The proceeds serve to fund any and all of the following: Tuition for students whose families wish for an education in the Jesuit tradition but cannot afford it • Salaries for teaching chairs • Retirement fund for teachers • Education initiatives • Athletic events

We are very grateful to our sponsors who helped us breach the 1.6 million USD mark in 2013. This has allowed us to assist over 550 students in need.
