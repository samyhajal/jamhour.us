Philippe Jabre

Philippe is the Founder and CIO of Jabre Capital Partners, a Geneva based Investment Management company which manages approximately $2bn in assets and employs 50 professionals. Following his Notre Dame de Jamhour 1977 Baccalaureate and MBA at Columbia University in 1982, Philippe worked in the asset management industry in New York, Paris and London and built a strong track record mainly in the Convertible Bonds and Global Equities asset classes. Philippe was awarded numerous industry awards for outstanding performance throughout his career: among others, Jabre Capital Partners won the Eurohedge Award for Management Firm of the Year - 2013, and best fund of the year for the year 2009.
