Jamhour Alumni U.S., Inc. is a U.S.-based, non-profit corporation whose mission is to arrange, sponsor and conduct fundraising events – and collect donations and charitable contributions – in favor of Collège Notre Dame de Jamhour located in Jamhour, Lebanon.

The proceeds serve to fund any and all of the following: Tuition for students whose families wish for an education in the Jesuit tradition but cannot afford it Salaries for teaching chairs • Retirement fund for teachers • Education initiatives • Athletic events
